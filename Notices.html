The View Finder - Season - V

We are very glad to inform you that,Film & Photography club is going to organize a platform for photography enthusiasts to express their talent & skills in photography. This semester we are introducing The View Finder - Season - V. All photographers from the Department of CSE are condiraly invited to this event. Any Students of CSE department (Regular + ex students) and the honorable Faculty members from CSE department can participate. For your convenience we strongly recommend you to go through the event details and registration procedure and of course don’t forget to read the "Terms & Conditions".

Submission Schedule

Submission Starts: 2nd April, 2022
Submission Closed: 10th April, 2022
Exhibition Date: 17th & 18th April, 2022
Venue: UAP-7th floor(Void- Department of CSE)

Event Details

Photography Section 1: Single Photos(DSLR/SLR)
Category Open:

# The Photographs must be taken by DSLR or SLR.
# Each participant can submit a maximum of 3 photos.
# Color and Black & White both will be accepted.
# Over processed photographs will not be accepted

Photography Section 2: Mobiography
Category: Open:

# The Photographs must be taken by smartphone, tablet, or pad.
# Each participant can submit a maximum of 3 photos.
# Color and Black & White both will be accepted.
# Over-processed photographs will not be accepted.
Photography Section 3: UAP Diary(any device)
# Each participant can submit a maximum of 3 photos.
# Color and Black & White both will be accepted.
# Over-processed photographs will not be accepted.
# Manipulation will be accepted only for conceptual photos.

Renaming Photos

Procedures for Renaming Photos are as follows:
NB: The participant's submission will not be accepted if he/she failed to send Email with the .doc file.
For Section 1, Section 2, and Section 3:

1. Renaming the Photos:
Each (single) photo should follow this naming convention-
'Photo Title_Photography Section No._Participants Name_type(Regular / ex-student/ faculty)_Registration No._Mobile No._Serial No.'
For example:
'Lifeline_Section 1_Sakib_Regular _181XXXXX_+88018XXXXXXXX_1' (Regular Student)
'LifePreraHin_Section 3_Irfan_ex-student _141XXXXX_+88015XXXXXXXX_1' (Ex-Student)
'PeraNaiChill_Section 2_Arnob_faculty _141XXXXX_+88019XXXXXXXX_1' (Faculty)

2. Resolution:
Must be in JPEG or JPG format and must be at least 3000 pixels on the longer side. The minimum resolution is 300dpi.

3. The .doc File:
The email must contain a .doc file with the required information:
Participant’s Name
Photo Title with description
Department
Registration No.
Contact Number
Email address
NB: Borders, Watermarks, signatures, logos are strictly prohibited.
Photographs have to be submitted through the following email:****fpc.cse@uap-bd.edu****
