About US

Film & Photography Club

FPC has been inaugurated in 27 March, 2017. This club is created to give a platform to the students who are interested in photography and film making. They find a platform where they can show their talent. What is the relation between FPC and CSE? It’s a common question from general mass. The answer would be there’s always a difference between passion and profession, film/photography is all about passion. Our students can experiment their ideas and would like to have some fun while working with FPC. This club motivates students to learn and share new ideas and techniques with fellows with similar interest and imagination power through film and photography.
